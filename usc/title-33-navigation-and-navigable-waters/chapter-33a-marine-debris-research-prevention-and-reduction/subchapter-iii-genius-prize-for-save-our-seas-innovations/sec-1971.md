### §1971. Definitions

In this subchapter:

#### (1) Prize competition

The term "prize competition" means the competition for the award of the Genius Prize for Save Our Seas Innovations established under section 1972 of this title.

#### (2) Secretary

The term "Secretary" means the Secretary of Commerce.

(Pub. L. 109–449, §121, formerly Pub. L. 116–224, title I, §121, Dec. 18, 2020, 134 Stat. 1081; renumbered Pub. L. 109–449, §121, by Pub. L. 119–65, §4(a), Dec. 26, 2025, 139 Stat. 1989.)
