### §1662a. Repealed. Pub. L. 118–232, §1, Jan. 4, 2025, 138 Stat. 2834

Section, Pub. L. 98–213, §12, Dec. 8, 1983, 97 Stat. 1462, provided that amendments or modifications to the constitution of American Samoa, as approved pursuant to Executive Order 10264 as in effect January 1, 1983, could be made only by Congress.
