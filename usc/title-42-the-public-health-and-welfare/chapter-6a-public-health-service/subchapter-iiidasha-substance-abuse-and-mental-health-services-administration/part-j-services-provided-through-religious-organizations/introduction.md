### Part J—Services Provided Through Religious Organizations

  

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Pub. L. 119–44, title I, §107(a)(2), Dec. 1, 2025, 139 Stat. 674, redesignated this part, which was formerly part G of title V of act July 1, 1944, as part J. Another part G of title V of act July 1, 1944, is classified to part G (§290hh et seq.) of this subchapter.
