### §410iii–5. Administration

The Secretary shall administer the Park in accordance with this subchapter and the provisions of law generally applicable to units of the National Park System, including—

(1) sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 [1](#410iii-5_1_target) of this title; and

(2) sections 461 to 467 [1](#410iii-5_1_target) of this title.

(Pub. L. 107–373, §7, Dec. 19, 2002, 116 Stat. 3106.)

#### References in Text

Sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, referred to in par. (1), were repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. For disposition of sections, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.

Sections 461 to 467 of this title, referred to in par. (2), were repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. For disposition of sections, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54.
