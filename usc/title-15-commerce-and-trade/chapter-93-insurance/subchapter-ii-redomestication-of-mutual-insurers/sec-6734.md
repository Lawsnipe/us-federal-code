### §6734. Other provisions

#### (a) Judicial review

The appropriate United States district court shall have exclusive jurisdiction over litigation arising under this section [1](#6734_1_target) involving any redomesticating or redomesticated insurer.

#### (b) Severability

If any provision of this section,[1](#6734_1_target) or the application thereof to any person or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of the section,[1](#6734_1_target) and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.

(Pub. L. 106–102, title III, §314, Nov. 12, 1999, 113 Stat. 1420.)

#### References in Text

This section, referred to in text, probably should be a reference to this subtitle, meaning subtitle B (§§311–316) of title III of Pub. L. 106–102, which is classified generally to this subchapter.
