### §735. Adoption of new constitution and bylaws

Upon written request of the tribal council,[1](#735_1_target) the Secretary shall hold an election for the members of the tribe for the purpose of adopting a new constitution and bylaws in accordance with section 476 of this title.

(Pub. L. 100–89, title II, §205, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 671.)

[1 So in original. Probably should be "Tribal Council,".](#735_1)
