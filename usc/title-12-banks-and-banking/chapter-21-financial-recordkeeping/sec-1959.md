### §1959. Administrative procedure

The administrative procedure and judicial review provisions of subchapter II of chapter 5 and chapter 7 of title 5 shall apply to all proceedings under this chapter, section 1829b of this title, and section 1730d [1](#1959_1_target) of this title.

([Pub. L. 91–508, title I, §129, Oct. 26, 1970, 84 Stat. 1118](/statviewer.htm?volume=84&page=1118).)

#### References in Text

Section 1730d of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–73, title IV, §407, Aug. 9, 1989, 103 Stat. 363](/statviewer.htm?volume=103&page=363).
